ZXFM: A Retro Football Management Sim

Getting Started

Customize your league: Use the 'R' key to rename teams and change their colors. Or just press ENTER to start.

Managing Your Team

Team Selection

  • View player information: Name, position (G=Goalkeeper, D=Defender, M=Midfielder, S=Striker), rating, and value.
  • Rename players with the 'N' key.
  • Manage injuries: Injured players are marked in red and can't play. Use the 'Move' option to replace them.

Main Menu

  • Move: Swap players between positions (e.g., defender for another defender).
  • Names: Rename players.
  • Save: Save your game progress.
  • Table: View the current league table and rename teams.
  • Buy: Purchase players from the transfer market.
  • Sell: Sell players to generate funds.
  • Play: Start a match.

Playing a Match

  • Your team's rating determines your chances of winning.
  • The 'vidiprinter' displays match events like goals and time.
  • After the match, the league table is updated.

End of Season

After 40 weeks, the season ends. Restart the game for a new season.

Troubleshooting

If the game crashes, type "GOTO 3" and press ENTER to resume.

Additional Tips

  • Aim for a high team rating by buying talented players and managing injuries.
  • Use the transfer market wisely to strengthen your team.
  • Rename players you buy if you want a realistic team sheet.
  • The ‘Save’ feature doesn’t work online yet.

Enjoy your football management experience on the ZX Spectrum!
